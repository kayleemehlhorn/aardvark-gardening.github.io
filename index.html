A Little Bit About Me

Hello! My name is Kaylee Mehlhorn. I am a solo start-up gardener looking to help you grow and maintain your garden(s) throughout the seasons! I am based in Whitefish but can service areas within the Flathead Valley.

I have been in the gardening business for 10 years, working for multiple different companies across different states. I have gardened in Minnesota(where I am from), Utah, Oregon, and now Montana. I am a hard working individual eager to make your garden come to life!
